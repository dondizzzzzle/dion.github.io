This is an all-in-one homepage to store my academic projects, namely my compilation of the history of 'pataphysics, a proto-absurdist philosophy that inspired Surrealism, Dadaism, the Theatre of the Absurd, and other overtly unserious or satirical schools of thought.

My goal is to track the development of 'pataphysical ideas through space and time, as well as its influence on literature, art, phiilosophy, and science. Additionally, I would like to confirm and put a name to as many 'pataphysicians as possible.
